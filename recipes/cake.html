Cake

Description

Cake is a sweet and soft dessert that people often eat at birthdays and parties. It is made from simple things like flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and baking powder. When you bake it, the batter grows and turns into a light, fluffy treat. Cakes can have different flavors like chocolate, vanilla, or fruit.

Cake is fun because you can decorate it in many ways. You can put frosting, sprinkles, or fruit on top. Some cakes have layers with cream or jam inside. Cakes can be fancy or simple, but they are always tasty and make celebrations special.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Oven and Pan
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
  3. Cream Butter and Sugar
  4. Add Eggs and Vanilla
  5. Combine Wet and Dry
  6. Bake the Cake
